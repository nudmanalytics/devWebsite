2022 NUDM Emcee Application

Thank you so much for your interest in being the Emcees for
Northwestern University Dance Marathon 2022!

This is an integral role to the success of NUDM, and we thank you for considering to spend your year working with us. There is an audition process for the role of Emcee, and applicants must sign up and audition as a pair or trio. Please work together to complete the following written application in a PDF document before your first-round audition.

The Application

Your combined application is due by Wednesday, September 22nd at 11:59pm. Please email a PDF version of your application titled FirstNames-Emcee-App to our Productions Co-Chairs, Gillian Finnegan and Kate Tadesse, at productions@nudm.org. In addition to the application questions, please submit a video (approx: 2 minutes) showcasing your personality, humor, and relationship with NUDM. Include the video file titled FirstNames-Emcee-Video in the email you send with your written application (.mp4 & .mov files are great, as well as anything shared on Drive!).

You are also required to do a live audition to complete your application; these will take place Thursday September 23rd - if you cannot attend on these days, we can make other arrangements. To schedule an audition, please fill out this Calendly with the submission of your application. If none of the time slots work for you or if you would like to audition virtually, please reach out to Gillian and Kate at productions@nudm.org.

Prospective emcees can audition as many times as they would like through their years at Northwestern, but please note that once a pair or trio is selected to be the NUDM emcees, they will be ineligible to reapply for the role in future years in order to ensure that as many members of the NUDM community as possible are able to hold this position. If you have any questions about the role or the audition process, please feel free to reach out to Gillian and Kate at productions@nudm.org! We can’t wait to see what you’ve got!
